Our Facilities

World Class Library

The libraries at each of the campuses have spacious reading halls, periodical centres, group discussion rooms and online database browsing areas.

All cafeterias have menus that are designed by a team of professional dieticians, and offer nutritious and wholesome food to ensure a well-balanced diet. Fresh fruit juices, milkshakes, ice creams sundaes and even midnight snacks are available in these cafeteria.

Imparting Knowledge

All our campuses have state-of-the-art computer centres that provide computing facilities comprising of the latest computer machines linked to a wide range of software, communication and print services.
